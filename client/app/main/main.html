What is a wedding? Two people in love who, in front of all of the humans who are so important that they couldn’t imagine proclaiming something so powerful without having those people there to witness them vowing to spend all of their days and nights loving each other more than anything. Easy peasy….until you start thinking about everyone who matters to you and how each of those humans would make the most important day of their lives (so far) even more fantastic.

Meagan is from the Chicago-land area and her people have spread through the mid-west, the south, the planet... Kyle is from all over the South and his people are similarly spread out. And then the two of them left the homeland half a decade ago, moved to Los Angeles and built a network of friends and family out here.

Before reading further, see upper right hand corner to sign up for emails to keep up-to-date on essential wedding things and the to-be-announced party dates. We promise we won’t sell your email address!
