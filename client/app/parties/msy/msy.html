CRAWFISH!

Crawfish (also known as “crayfish” and “crawdad”) is a Louisiana delicacy. Late January through April is the best time to harvest and consume these delicious little critters which is why we are holding this party a bit before the actual nuptials—we want everyone to have the best experience with lots of beer, mudbugs, and tons of fun. Crawfish are prepared in many batches so prepare for a marathon and be excited and/or warned: the batches get spicier as the party goes on. Getting excited for crawfish? Check out the video below to learn how to eat them like a boss!

Suggested Weekend Gear:

A Personal Fan Being O2Cool is O2 important!
Kayak A site most are familiar with and a good baseline for prices. Not the best deals but definitely not the worst.
AirBnB A modern lodging concept where you get to rent out someone’s room, apartment, or house instead of a hotel. Typically way less expensive than hotels and you get to stay in actual neighborhoods. Be sure to get everything set up way ahead of time—there is a registration process and small background check. (Meagan has used this several times and prefers this to hotels when spending a few days in a new city.)
Priceline Check out hotels in Metarie (close to the party) or Uptown/Garden District/Downtown (to be in New Orleans). With Priceline, you can usually book day-of with no issues and sometimes get last-minute deals.
Uber We know, it’s not a place to stay. However, it’s worth mentioning when you’re considering travel arrangements. You can easily taxi to wherever you’re staying and Uber (order and pay through your smartphone) wherever you want. WAY better than a rental car: 1) you almost always save money 2) the Uber driver is always your designated driver.
