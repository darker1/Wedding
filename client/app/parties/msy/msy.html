The Big Easy:

This is Kyle's old stomping grounds. It is where he is from, and it is the best place to throw a party! We will be holding a crawfish boil and celebrating the entire weekend! Prepare for non-stop fun, trips to the french quarter, quite a few drinks, GREAT food and even Drive Through Daiquiris (Yes that is really a thing)!

CRAWFISH!

Crawfish is a Louisiana "delicacy." Most parties between the end of January and the beginning of June showcase lots of beer, mudbugs, and tons of fun. Prepare for outdoor fun games like cornhole, beerpong, and flip-cup. As for the crawfish themselves, we usually make many batches over the course of a day - the latter batches usually get spicier. Getting excited for crawfish? Check out the video below to find out how to eat them!

Where to stay.

New Orleans can be a tricky place to figure out where to stay.

Kayak This is the base line for prices. Not the best, definitely not the worst.
AirBnB A new age concept where you rent out someone's place for a day or two. Usually on the more inexpensive end - be sure to get everything set up way ahead of time as there may be a small background check involved.
Priceline Check out hotels in Metairie (to be close to the party) or uptown/garden district/downtown (to be in New Orleans). With Priceline, you can usually book day of with no issues.
